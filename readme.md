## ✨ Features

- 🔒 **Secure Storage** — All data is safely stored in your **Google Drive**.
- ⚡ **Fast & Easy** — Designed for simplicity and speed.
- 🔄 **Cross-Platform Access** — Available on **Windows**, **Linux**, **iOS**, **Mac**, and **Android**.
- 💾 **Offline Access** — Cached notes for seamless offline usage.
- 💰 **Free Forever** — No hidden costs or subscriptions.

---

## 📚 **How It Works**

1. ✅ All data is automatically saved on your **Google Drive**.
2. 📝 Create notes, save documents, and access your files **from any device**.
3. 🔗 Open any file directly via **Google Drive** — Just press **"OPEN IN GOOGLE DRIVE"**.
4. 🔐 Only people with your direct permission can access specific files.
5. ❌ You can remove the app from your **Google Account** at any time —  
   Your data will remain safe and sound in your **Google Drive**.
